Installing the app

  1. On your Android phone, tap Download APK (or scan the QR code).
  2. Open the downloaded .apk file.
  3. If prompted, allow your browser to install unknown apps, then confirm.

These apps are distributed directly rather than through the Play Store, so Android will ask you to confirm the install. The APKs are hosted on my public GitHub releases.
