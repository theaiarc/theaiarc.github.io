Our Story

Started in a Garage, Built for a Community

NEXUS began in 2019 with four friends, six PCs, and a shared frustration: there was nowhere local to play on serious hardware with people who actually cared about the game.

Today we run 40 battlestations, console lounges, full-motion sim rigs, and a café that keeps the energy up till close. But the mission hasn't changed — we're still just building the spot we always wanted to play at.
