## Trademark / logo cleanup

The original draft used several stock photos that prominently showed **third-party
brand logos** (Levi's jeans, Salvatore Ferragamo bag, ALDO heels, Vans/Nike
sneakers). Selling such items as our own "LUXE" products risks trademark
infringement and passing-off. Those images were **replaced with logo-free
photos**. The current product images contain no recognizable brand marks.

## Residual risks to review before going live

1. **People's likenesses.** Some photos show identifiable people
   (`dress.jpg`, `tee.jpg`, `coat.jpg`, `trousers.jpg`, `cat-women.jpg`,
   `cat-men.jpg`). Unsplash does **not** guarantee model releases. Using a
   recognizable person to advertise products commercially can raise
   right-of-publicity/privacy issues. For a real launch, prefer photos with model
   releases or product-only / faceless shots.
2. **"LUXE" brand name** is placeholder content. Check trademark availability in
   your jurisdiction before trading under it.
3. **Demo copy & policies.** Marketing claims ("free shipping over $100",
   "30-day returns", "secure checkout", newsletter "10% off") are placeholders.
   Make sure your real policies, prices, and tax/shipping rules are accurate and
   legally compliant (consumer law, privacy/cookie notices, etc.) before selling.
4. **Payment form** in `checkout.html` is a non-functional demo and must not
   collect real card data without a PCI-compliant payment processor.
