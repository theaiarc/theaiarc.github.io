Since 2014

Beauty, with heart

Blush Beauty Salon began with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to feel beautiful and cared for. What started as a single styling chair has grown into a beloved studio with a team of fifteen talented artists.

We've stayed true to our roots — combining genuine warmth with serious skill. Whether you're here for a quick trim or a full bridal transformation, you'll always get our full attention, honest advice, and a result you'll love.
