Our Story

Beautiful homes aren't about trends — they're about how a space makes you feel.

Since 2014

From a market stall to a styling studio

Maison Decor started at a weekend market with a single table of hand-thrown ceramics. What began as a love of beautiful objects grew into a curated store and a full interior styling studio.

Today we work with makers across the globe and with homeowners around the corner — pairing artisan craft with timeless, liveable design.

  • 500+ homes styled since 2014
  • 60+ independent makers supported
  • Carbon-neutral shipping
